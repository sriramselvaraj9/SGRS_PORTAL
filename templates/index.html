Why Use Our System?

Secure & Confidential

Your data is protected with secure storage. Submit anonymously if you prefer.

Automated Routing

Grievances are automatically routed to the concerned department for swift action.

Real-Time Tracking

Track the status of your complaint in real-time with a unique ticket ID.

Dashboard & Analytics

Authorities get comprehensive dashboards to monitor and resolve cases efficiently.

Escalation Mechanism

Unresolved grievances can be escalated to higher authorities automatically.

Feedback System

Rate the resolution process and help us improve our services.

Grievance Categories

Academic

Issues related to curriculum, faculty, grading, attendance, and academic policies.

Administrative

Problems with admissions, fee payments, certificates, and administrative processes.

Hostel

Complaints about hostel facilities, mess, maintenance, and living conditions.

Examination

Issues with exam schedules, results, revaluation, and examination conduct.

How It Works

1

Submit

2

3

4

Feedback
